Restorative

In our busy and stressful world, we all need a little more time for ourselves, a little more time to soften and absorb, to relax and release, and to simply let go and become more present. We all need restoration in our lives! Whether we feel burned out from overworking our bodies, mentally exhausted from our job or from parenting, or just constantly stressed and tired, we must make time for renewal and self-care to maintain our good health and well-being.

Restorative Yoga for Relaxation and Rejuvenation is a balanced sequence of restorative poses for relief of stress, anxiety and tension. This course offers complete video instructions for each pose of the sequence. The use of props for each pose is shown in detail so that anyone, regardless of previous experience, can follow and experience the transformative effect and deep relaxation that restorative yoga has to offer.

Yoga teachers will learn to assist students in restorative yoga poses; including assisting in prop set up, modifications and adjustments for each student's individual needs.

This course is offered by Satya Greenstone. In the Tradition and Lineage of Sri Swami Satchidananda; Satya has been practicing and teaching yoga for 46 years.

Restore yourself to your natural state of balance through the gentle supported poses of Restorative Yoga for Relaxation and Rejuvenation
